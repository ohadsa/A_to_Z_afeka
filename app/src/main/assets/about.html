MOVIEAPP

Android App only for educational purpose

Patterns and Third party libraries applied

  1. MVVM
  2. Compose
  3. Jetpack (Livedata, ViewModel, Pagination, Navigation, Preference)
  4. Hilt Dependency injection
  5. Coroutines and flows
  6. Retrofit2
  7. Single Activity Pattern
  8. Material Design

Features

  1. Movies lists, Top rated , upcoming, Today's hot
  2. Search all Movies That exist
  3. Favorites
  4. WishList
  5. details and rating
